PunCake

Description

Pancakes, man I fucking love pancakes. There's no better dessert out there, savory or sweet, so unpredictable.
The origin of pancakes dates back hundreds and thousands of years, our ancestors have been creating and evolving similar dishes.
Such as the indigenous of Australia would utilise cooking batter over ash in their camp fires to make damper. Pancakes are a livelihood!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk & butter
  • Egg

Pancake From The Get-Go!

  1. Sift your dry ingredients together
  2. Create a foxhole of flour, stirring wet ingredients inside
  3. Scoop and apply batter to cooking surface
  4. Cook 2-3 minutes each side
  5. Cook until brown on both sides
  6. Enjoy your bloodclaat panquakes

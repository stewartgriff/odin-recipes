donut

Description

Donuts, fried goods who doesn't love them? Biting into one fresh out the oven is like a kiss of death, many would do egregious shameful deeds to get their hands on their favorable donut.

Such a versatile snack, coming in various forms of flavors and even designs as we're not subjugated to eating them in the traditional form of 'rings'. Have no shame when you bite into these, especially when pink, frosted and sprinkled...

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Large egg
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Fresh strawberries: 1/2 cup

GLAZE

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Powdered sugar
  • Milk
  • Lemon zest

Steps

  1. Whisk Ingredients

    2. Large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt; set aside. Seperate bowl whisk egg, sugar, oil, sour cream and vanilla till combined.

  2. Transfer Batter to Pan

    3. Evenly transfer and fill donut cavities in your mould, 3/4 full.

  3. Bake The Donuts!

    4. Bake for 12-15 minutes, let donuts cool in pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool fully through.

  4. Strawberry Glaze

    5. Strawberries in a blender till liquid, transferring to a small bowl mix with powdered sugar and lemon zest. Whisk thoroughly until smooth, add milk for desired consistency.

  5. CREATION

    6. Dip your donuts once cool into the GLAZE, allow the glaze to harden, bite into and ENJOY!
